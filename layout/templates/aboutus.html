ABOUT US

Kheta Ram Jakhar is a distinguished PWD "AA" Class Contractor, recognized for his expertise in delivering large-scale civil engineering and infrastructure projects. As an "AA" Class registered contractor the highest tier of enlistment with the Public Works Department he holds the distinct qualification to tender for and execute government and private projects of unlimited value.

Core Expertise:

  • Government Tendering: Eligible to bid on high-value government contracts across the state without financial caps.
  • Project Management: End-to-end execution of turnkey projects, ensuring strict adherence to quality and safety standards.
